Room 02 About the Artist
✦ Design Museum • Artist Plaque

Sp Designs (Rejoice Okorie)

Graphic Designer & Visual Storyteller

With over 4 years of immersive design experience, Sp Designs transforms concepts into compelling visual narratives. Specialising in brand identity, digital design, and visual communication, her work bridges the gap between art and strategy — creating designs that don't just look beautiful, but speak volumes.

Location Nigeria
Available For Freelance & Collaborations
Philosophy "Design is not just what it looks like — it's how it makes you feel."

Design Journey

2020

The Beginning

Discovered a passion for graphic design and began self-learning Adobe Creative Suite and the principles of visual communication.

2021

First Clients

Built first brand identities for local businesses. Expanded into social media design and digital marketing creatives.

2022

UI/UX Exploration

Ventured into UI design and Figma, creating app interfaces and web prototypes with a focus on user experience.

2023

International Reach

Worked with international clients on premium branding campaigns, poster designs, and multi-platform digital strategies.

2024–Present

The Design Museum Opens

Curating a growing collection of award-worthy work — pushing creative boundaries across all design disciplines.
